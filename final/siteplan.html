Nexora

Nexora Books is a digital library website that allows users to explore and discover books online. Users can browse a wide variety of titles, view book covers, read brief descriptions, and filter books by genre or language. The site simulates a rental system where users can add books to a personal reading list

Scenarios

  • are the most recent books available?
  • can I search for a book by title?
  • can I just rent a book instead of buying it??
