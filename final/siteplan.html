Nexora

This project will have the functionality of an online movie rental platform. Movie data—including titles, genres, prices, descriptions, and images—is stored and loaded into the interface using Js. Users can search for movies by title or filter them by genre, then view detailed information in a popup modal.

Scenarios

  • are the most recent movies available?
  • can I search for a movie by title?
  • can I just rent a movie instead of buying it??
